Watal Report on Digital Payments

Watal Report

Watal Report is report of committee under chairmanship of Shri Ratan P. Watal, Former Finance Secretary and Principal Advisor, NITI Ayog. The committee which was constituted by Ministry of Finance, Government of India, submitted a report titled Medium Term Recommendations to Strengthen Digital Payments Ecosystem Report Watal Committee Report Feedback is welcome on the report at currency-dea@gov.in within 15 days of release of the report (Jan 11, 2017). CashlessConsumer will try to simplify policy recommendations, give you various perspectives to submit your feedback soon. If you would like to volunteer, contact us cashlessconsumerin@gmail.com
